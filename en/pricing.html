Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the quarterly consultation?

Our quarterly consultation includes a comprehensive review of your domain portfolio, technical support for domain-related issues, and strategic advice on domain management and SaaS implementation.

How does the domain emergency service work?

Our domain emergency service provides priority support for critical domain issues such as expiration problems, DNS configuration, website downtime, and security concerns. Annual partners receive expedited response times.

Can I upgrade from quarterly to annual plan?

Yes, you can upgrade at any time. We'll prorate the remaining value of your quarterly plan toward the annual partnership.

Do you offer refunds?

We stand behind our services. If you're not satisfied with our consultation, please contact us within 7 days, and we'll work to resolve your concerns or provide a refund.

How do I schedule my consultation time?

After signing up, you'll receive access to our scheduling system where you can book your consultation at a time that works for you. Annual partners have priority scheduling options.
